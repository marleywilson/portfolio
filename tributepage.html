Eric Barone

Eric Barone aka Concerened Ape

a picture of Eric Barone

Eric Barone or Concerened Ape started working on his most famous project Stardew valley in 2012 and released it in 2016. He worked on Stardew Valley completly by himself and he was put on the Forbes "30 Under 30: Games" list.

He made a game in 2008 witch was called 17CF Quest witch was made to promote a music group 17 Colorful Feathers. Next in 2011 he made a comic book series called Wumbus world. while working on Stardew valley he took a small break and made the mobile game Air pear. finially in 2016 he released Stardew valley and later even a Stardew valley themed board game. He is currently working on a new game Haunted Chocolatier.
